9 /** FairPrimaryGenerator.h
10  *@author V.Friese <v.friese@gsi.de>
11  *
12 The FairPrimaryGenerator is responsible for the handling of the MC input.
13 Several input generators can be registered to it; these have to be
14 derived from the FairGenerator class. The FairPrimaryGenerator defines
15 position and (optionally) smearing of the primary vertex.
16 This class should be instantised only once.
17 
18 Modified 05.06.07: add a method DoTracking(Bool_t) to be able to switch on/off
