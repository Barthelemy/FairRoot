14 /** FairStack.h
15  *@author D.Bertini <d.bertini@gsi.de>
16  *@author V.Friese <v.friese@gsi.de>
17  **
18  ** Version 14/06/07 by V. Friese
19  **
20  ** This class handles the particle stack for the transport simulation.
21  ** For the stack FILO functunality, it uses the STL stack. To store
22  ** the tracks during transport, a TParticle arry is used.
23  ** At the end of the event, tracks satisfying the filter criteria
24  ** are copied to a FairMCTrack array, which is stored in the output.
25  **
26  ** The filtering criteria for the output tracks are:
27  ** - primary tracks are stored in any case.
28  ** - secondary tracks are stored if they have a minimal number of
29  ** points (sum of all detectors) and a minimal energy, or are the
30  **
31  ** The storage of secondaries can be switched off.
32  ** The storage of all mothers can be switched off.
33  ** By default, the minimal number of points is 1 and the energy cut is 0.
34  **/
