15 /** MyProjStack.h
16  **
17  ** This class handles the particle stack for the transport simulation.
18  ** For the stack FILO functunality, it uses the STL stack. To store
19  ** the tracks during transport, a TParticle array is used.
20  ** At the end of the event, tracks satisfying the filter criteria
21  ** are copied to a MyProjMCTrack array, which is stored in the output.
22  **
23  ** The filtering criteria for the output tracks are:
24  ** - primary tracks are stored in any case.
25  ** - secondary tracks are stored if they have a minimal number of
26  ** points (sum of all detectors) and a minimal energy, or are the
27  **
28  ** The storage of secondaries can be switched off.
29  ** The storage of all mothers can be switched off.
30  ** By default, the minimal number of points is 1 and the energy cut is 0.
31  **/
